TechMeetup is a monthly excuse for developers and the tech community around Scotland to eat pizza, drink beer (or juice!) and learn new stuff from each other. No name badges, no sales pitches, just informative presentations from your peers and a chance to meet some of the tech community around you.

We're not centred around specific languages or technologies; we believe there's stuff to learn from mixing everyone together. There's no need to register or cost to attend — just find out when the next one is one near you and show up!
